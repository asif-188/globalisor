KYC & AML

Dedicated module for drafting resolutions using Generative AI, trained on your preferred legal templates and style guides.

Automated reminders, newsletters, and a searchable blog database. Integrated client service ticket mechanism for support.

Invoice generation with Stripe and multi-provider integration. Automated collection reminders to ensure timely payments.

Integrated E-Sign for documents and Bizfile data extraction to automatically populate client profiles without manual entry.
